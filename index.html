Project Overview

Research Objective

Develop machine learning models to predict air quality categories in Yogyakarta based on pollutant concentrations (PM2.5, PM10, SO2, CO, O3, NO2).

Methodology

Comparison of two popular machine learning techniques: Random Forest (ensemble method) and Support Vector Machine (SVM) for classification.
